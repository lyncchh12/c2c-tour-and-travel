rabdantse

Rabdentse was once the capital of the former Kingdom of Sikkim and is now a popular tourist attraction in the state. Located near the town of Pelling, Rabdentse is known for its historic ruins and stunning panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. Visitors can explore the ancient palace and temples, including the ruins of the Chogyal's palace and the Rabdentse ruins. The site also features several walking trails that lead through the forested hills and provide a glimpse into Sikkim's rich history and culture. Rabdentse is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in history, culture, and stunning natural beauty.
