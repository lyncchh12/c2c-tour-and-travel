Gurudongmar

Gurudongmar Lake is a high-altitude lake located in North Sikkim, India. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains, the lake is considered sacred by both Hindus and Buddhists and is one of the highest lakes in the world, situated at an altitude of 17,800 feet. The crystal clear waters of the lake are said to have healing properties, and visitors can enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding peaks, including Mount Siniolchu and Kangchengyao. Gurudongmar Lake is accessible only during certain months of the year due to heavy snowfall, and visitors require a permit from the local authorities. It is a must-visit destination for those seeking adventure, natural beauty, and spiritual experiences.
