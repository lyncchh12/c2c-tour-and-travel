ravangla

Ravangla is a small town located in the southern region of Sikkim, known for its picturesque landscapes, peaceful atmosphere, and rich cultural heritage. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains and lush green forests, Ravangla offers stunning views of the Himalayan range and is a popular destination for trekking and hiking enthusiasts. The town is also home to several Buddhist monasteries, including the Ralang Monastery and the Buddha Park, which features a towering statue of Lord Buddha. Visitors can also explore the local market, which offers a variety of handicrafts and souvenirs. Ravangla is a perfect destination for those seeking peace, tranquility, and natural beauty.
